The photographer

Hi, I'm Ellinor

I'm the photographer behind Smith & Light Co. — a traveling portrait photographer rooted in the belief that the quietest, most ordinary moments are worth preserving.

I specialize in relaxed, natural portraits for families, couples, and seniors across the Louisville and Southern Indiana area. I come to you — whether that's a golden-hour field, your favorite local spot, a special venue, or your own living room in December.

My sessions are unhurried, warm, and genuinely fun — because the best photos happen when everyone forgets the camera is there.
